About Me

I’m a GIS student at Delta State University preparing for a career as a geospatial data scientist. I specialize in GIS programming, spatial analysis, and cartographic visualization, with skills in Python, ArcGIS, and remote sensing. I’ve applied these tools to projects ranging from disaster response simulations to community-focused mapping in the Mississippi Delta. My goal is to transform complex spatial data into clear insights and practical solutions that support decision-making and public impact.

Relevant courses: GIS Programming • Remote Sensing • Cartography & Visualization • Case Studies.
Interests: geospatial data science, disaster response, and community mapping.
